Easy Fried Rice

A quick, delicious fried rice recipe perfect for Sunday meals.

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: 3–4 people
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked rice (preferably chilled)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 table spoon soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and scramble the eggs.
  2. Add onions and garlic, sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry.
  4. Add the cooked rice and soy sauce.
  5. Mix well and cook for 3 minutes.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy.

Tips

Use cold rice for best texture. Adjust soy sauce to your taste.
